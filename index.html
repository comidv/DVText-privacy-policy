DVText – Privacy Policy

This privacy policy outlines how DVText – Text Reader (TTS) handles user data. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

1. Data We Collect

DVText collects only the essential information needed to provide core features and ensure a smooth experience:

  • Email address – used as a unique identifier for account and subscription management
  • Device ID – for identifying the user's device and managing purchases
  • Subscription token – for verifying in-app purchases via Google Play

2. How We Use Your Data

The collected data is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • Managing subscriptions and verifying purchase status
  • Enabling one-tap Google sign-in via Firebase Authentication
  • Providing account-based features for subscribed users

No personal data is used for advertising or analytics purposes.

3. Web Browsing & Local Data

DVText includes a built-in web browser for reading aloud web pages. We do not collect any browsing activity, history, cookies, or web content. All such data remains on your device and is never sent to our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Firebase for authentication and secure cloud storage. Google Play is used for handling subscription purchases. Both services adhere to industry-standard security practices.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely in Firebase Firestore with restricted access. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties.

6. Account & Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by visiting: Account Deletion Request.

7. Age Restrictions

DVText is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. Continued use of DVText after changes means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact
